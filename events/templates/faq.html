Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about EventZone. Quick, clear, and concise.

How do I create an event?

Navigate to your dashboard, click "Create Event," and fill in the details such as title, schedule, tickets, and permissions. You can preview before publishing.

Can I import attendees in bulk?

Yes! You can upload a CSV with attendee details or use our bulk import tool. Assign them to groups and customize registration fields easily.

How do automated reminders work?

You can schedule reminders and follow-ups for registered attendees. Choose templates, send via email, and track open rates and click-throughs.

Can I export event analytics?

Absolutely! All event metrics, including RSVPs, attendance, and engagement, can be exported as CSV or Excel files for stakeholders and reports.
